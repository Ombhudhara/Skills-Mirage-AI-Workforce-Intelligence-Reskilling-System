## 🛠️ System Architecture & Features

### 1. Market Intelligence Dashboard (Layer 1)
- **Live Scrape**: Trigger real-time data extraction from LinkedIn & Naukri using the top-right "Live Market Scrape" button.
- **Neural Demand Mapping**: View job growth across 10+ Indian cities.
- **Automation Risk Visualization**: High-fidelity charts showing which sectors are most vulnerable to AI.

### 2. Worker Intelligence Engine (Layer 2)
- **Neural Profile Analysis**: Analyze your current role, salary, and experience.
- **Vulnerability Scoring**: Get a detailed 0-100 risk score based on real-time hiring declines and AI mentions in job descriptions.
- **Skill Gap Detection**: Automatically detects missing skills required for your current role in the modern market.

### 3. Reskilling Pathfinder (Layer 3)
- **Growth Trajectory**: A week-by-week synthesis of how to transition to a new high-growth role.
- **Verified Course Synthesis**: Recommends top-rated courses from **NPTEL, SWAYAM, and PMKVY**.
- **Execution Pathway**: Direct deep-links to enroll in free, government-verified certificate programs.

### 4. Career Transitions Engine
- **Quantum Role Mapping**: Maps your current skills to 3+ high-growth alternative career paths.
- **Salary Upshift Projection**: Predicts salary growth (e.g., +120%) for each transition.
- **Neural Gap Analysis**: Identifies exactly which skills you need to learn to make the jump successfully.
